What are unbanned games?

Unbanned games, often called Unbanned games, are typically simple web-based games that can run in a browser without requiring special plugins or software. They are designed to be accessible on networks that might have strict firewalls, such as those found in schools or workplaces, which often block access to major gaming websites.
