About Fortz 🧱

Fortz is a highly ingenious and chaotic local 2-player action shooter that focuses on the blend of creative building and destructive physics. The objective is to control your small base in a split-screen arena, utilizing your limited resources to build a protective fort of blocks around your core, strategically place cannons on your fort, and then fire at your rival's fort to destroy their defenses and hit their core to win the round. The challenge is immense because every shot involves realistic physics, where a well-placed cannonball can destroy multiple blocks and cause the rival's fort to collapse entirely. The game’s strong point is the fusion of strategic building with chaotic, physics-based destruction. What sets Fortz Unbanned apart is the focus on structural engineering; a stable, well-designed fort that withstands high-impact shots is the key to victory. The comparison is strong to classics like Worms. A crucial strategic tip is to always build a strong, rounded base; a square, flat fort is easily defeated by shots aimed at the bottom corners, causing the entire structure to flip.

Game Features: Fortz

  • Chaotic, physics-based local 2-Player shooter action.
  • Unique mechanic of strategic fort building and destruction.
  • Requires high skill in aiming, power control, and structural strategy.
  • Focus on destroying the opponent's core base.
  • Simple, intuitive controls for building and shooting.

How to Play Fortz 🕹️

Playing Fortz Unbanned is designed for a shared keyboard. Player 1 typically uses WASD for movement and Spacebar for action. Player 2 uses the Arrow keys for movement and Enter/Ctrl for action. The core strategy is defense and siege. Build a fort that protects your core from all angles, and only place cannons that have a clear, direct angle on the weakest parts of your opponent's fort. Use the cannons to take out the rival's base supports, causing a massive, satisfying collapse.
